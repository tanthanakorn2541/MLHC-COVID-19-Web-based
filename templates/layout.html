A Multi-Layer Classification Model for COVID-19

The coronavirus disease of 2019 (COVID-19) has been declared a pandemic and raised worldwide concern. Lung inflammation and respiratory failure are commonly found in moderate to severe cases. Radiography or chest x-ray imaging is compulsory for the diagnosis and the interpretation is commonly performed by skilled medical specialists. In this work, we propose a new Computer-Aided Diagnosis (CADx) tool for classifying chest x-ray images of COVID-19 cases using Multi-Layer Hybrid Classification model (MLHC). The MLHC-COVID-19 consists of two layers, Layer I: Healthy and Non-Healthy and Layer II: COVID-19 and Non-COVID-19. (Image for Test)
